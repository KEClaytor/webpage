Fantasy Football Roster

I don't much care for fantasy football, but after being drafted into a league, I figured I might as well make the best of it. This script was how I chose my team. We were using a bididng system and this script suggests players and points based on past performance and predicted future performance (by suggested bidding cost).
But I apply an additional constraint that the players must not have an arrest record.
